About the store

Welcome to Movie Mall, the perfect haven for classic film lovers! Here, every corner is dedicated to the masterpieces that have shaped the history of cinema. Our collection spans from the icons of black-and-white cinema to the unforgettable films of the golden decades. If you're looking to relive the magic of the classics, our carefully curated selection is the place to find that movie that touches the heart and never goes out of style. Visit Movie Mall and bring home the magic of classic cinema!

Chinatown, 1974

Directed by Roman Polanski and starring Jack Nicholson and Faye Dunaway, "Chinatown" is a classic film noir that explores a complex plot of corruption, betrayal, and mystery in 1930s Los Angeles.

The Exorcist, 1973

Considered one of the scariest films ever made, "The Exorcist" directed by William Friedkin tells the story of a young girl possessed by a demonic entity and the efforts to exorcise her.

Die Hard, 1988

Directed by John McTiernan and starring Bruce Willis, "Die Hard" is one of the most iconic action films of all time. The story follows NYPD officer John McClane as he takes on terrorists in a high-rise building in Los Angeles.

Psycho, 1960

Directed by Alfred Hitchcock, "Psycho" is a landmark in the suspense genre, featuring a plot full of twists and one of the most famous scenes in film history (the shower murder).
